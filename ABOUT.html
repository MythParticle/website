About Us

Ian & Shan’s Literary House is a hybrid bookstore-library-café in Jamaica offering a curated global book collection, lending services, a back-to-school supply shop, a Bible & philosophy section, computer access, and a cozy café. We provide a welcoming environment for families, students, educators, creatives, and tourists.

Our Mission

To foster a love of reading, learning, and community through a hybrid bookstore-library-café space that nurtures creativity, literacy, and cultural engagement in Jamaica.

Our Vision

To become Jamaica’s premier destination for book lovers, students, educators, and families seeking both knowledge and leisure in one welcoming space.

Why Choose Ian & Shan’s Literary House?

  • Hybrid experience: Buy, borrow, read, and relax all under one roof.
  • Curated global and Caribbean book collections.
  • Membership perks including borrowing privileges and café discounts.
  • Special sections for children, students, and professionals.
  • Community-driven events, storytelling sessions, and workshops.
  • Quiet café & computer access for research and work.
