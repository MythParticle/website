Our Services

At Ian & Shan’s Literary House, we offer a wide range of services to foster a love for reading, learning, and community engagement.

Bookstore & Lending Library

Buy books or borrow from our curated collection. Specialty sections include Children, Academic, Bible & Spiritual, and Reference materials.

Children’s Area

Comfortable reading spaces and weekend storytelling sessions. Back-to-school supplies available.

Café

Coffee, tea, baked goods, and a quiet reading environment. Members enjoy exclusive discounts.

Computer Access

Public-use PCs with membership login or pay-per-hour access for research, study, and browsing.

Workshops & Events

Join author talks, writing workshops, book clubs, and community literacy events throughout the year.

Membership Perks

Members receive special access to limited edition books, café discounts, and priority booking for events.
